# The Customer Experience Roadmap

A strategic framework for transforming packaging distribution sales through AI-powered intelligence.

## Phase 1: Discover (Days 1-30)
**Outcome:** Fast onboarding, first value in days.
*   **The Wedge:** The 60-Second Interaction.
*   **Experience:** Reps use "Meeting Prep" to generate AI briefings.
*   **Metric:** Time to First Value < 3 Days.

## Phase 2: Adopt (Months 2-6)
**Outcome:** Weekly usage, habit formation.
*   **The Shift:** From "trying it out" to "can't live without it."
*   **Experience:** Post-Meeting Analyzer becomes the default follow-up tool.
*   **Metric:** 75%+ Weekly Active Users.

## Phase 3: Expand (Months 7-12)
**Outcome:** Multi-team, quantified ROI.
*   **The Scale:** Operations and Management join the platform.
*   **Experience:** Managers use dashboards to coach; Ops uses supplier matching.
*   **Metric:** 30%+ Revenue Growth per Rep.

## Phase 4: Automate (Months 13-24)
**Outcome:** Predictive intelligence, partnership.
*   **The Brain:** The system starts telling *you* what to do.
*   **Experience:** "Morning Intelligence Briefings" auto-generated for every rep.
*   **Metric:** 85%+ Predictive Accuracy.

## Phase 5: Platform (Month 24+)
**Outcome:** Ecosystem, category leadership.
*   **The Moat:** Defft.ai becomes the operating system for the industry.
*   **Experience:** Third-party apps and custom workflows via API.
*   **Metric:** 90%+ Platform Dependency.
